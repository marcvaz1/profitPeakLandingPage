ProfitPeak

Get Started

Open-source MIT Licensed.

Resume Upload 🛡️

Upload resumes in various formats (PDF, Word, etc.) to ProfitPeak for automatic extraction of relevant information.

Resume Analysis 🚀

Analyze resumes to extract key details such as skills, experience, education, and contact information.

Candidate Notifications 📈

Receive notifications when new candidates matching specified criteria become available, ensuring no missed hiring opportunities.

Candidate Search 🦉

Search for candidates based on specific criteria such as skills, experience level, and location.

Job Matching ✨

Match job seekers with relevant job opportunities from various sources, such as job boards and company websites.

Detailed Candidate Profiles 🧰

View detailed profiles of candidates, including extracted resume information and any additional documents uploaded.
